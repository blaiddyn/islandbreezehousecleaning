Services

All Rooms:

Dust & vacuum. Dust window sills, doors, picture frames, lamps, furniture, woodwork, shelves, and light dusting of baseboards. Vacuum carpets and wash floors throughout. Empty and clean wastebaskets.

Kitchen

Clean exterior of appliances, cabinets, table and chairs. Sanitize and scrub counter, sinks and back splashes. Interior of appliances can be cleaned for a small additional fee of $15.95 per appliance.

Living Room

All furniture dusted and wiped. All electronic equipment and knick-knacks dusted and cleaned. Carpet or area rugs vacuumed and wood floors washed.

Dining Room

All furniture including glass and wood tables dusted and polished. Carpet or area rugs vacuumed throughout.

Bedrooms

Complete dusting including mirrors, night tables, dressers and lamps. We can also change your bed linens- please request this at the time of booking.

Bathrooms

Clean, disinfect and deodorize showers, bathtubs, vanities, cabinets, mirrors, sinks and toilets. Wash and scrub floors, tile walls, and chrome polished.

Hallways

Baseboards wiped down, fingerprints washed from all woodwork, door frames and switch plates. Vacuum or wash floors throughout.
